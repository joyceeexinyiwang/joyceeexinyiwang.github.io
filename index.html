Joyce Wang 王心怡

Joyce is a technologist and upcoming Associate Product Manager at Schmidt Futures.

She is interested in #civic-media, #education, and the #arts.

A recent alumna of Carnegie Mellon University's B.C.S.A. program and the Fifth Year Scholar program, Joyce has been recognized for her work across the realms of computer science, theater, and community building. She has published research that explores creative applications of artificial intelligence; conducted an ethnographic study about journalism and social media; directed and performed in a theater performance; and led an initiative to improve the interdisciplinary education experience at CMU.

Joyce was born and raised in Beijing, China. She is about to move to New York City.
